The Town Hall

  • The Town Hall helps residents track and schedule upcoming events within their local community.
  • Events span a wide range of interests, including theatrical performances, social groups, conventions, and more.
  • Users can browse publicly hosted events on the main page and access tools to organize their own events.
  • The app addresses growing social isolation caused by increased reliance on online interactions.
  • Research consistently shows that social connection and interpersonal relationships are essential to human wellbeing.
  • By encouraging local gatherings, the platform helps people connect, build friendships, and strengthen their community.
