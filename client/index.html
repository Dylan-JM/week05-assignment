Welcome to The Town Hall. The Town Hall is a way to keep track and schedule events that are upcomming in your local community. Events range from theatrical performances to social groups to conventions (and everything imbetween). You can find public hosted events on our main page below, and have the tools to organize your own!

Why an online app? In the modern day, social isolation has proven to be a significant problem and more and more individuals are opting to be more online than ever before - this is both a pro and a con. Psychologists have proven for decades that human beings are social creatures, and our wellbeing craves social connection and interpersonal relationships. Being more and more online can limit people's wellbeing especially when it becomes a bigger dependant factor. So, why not have an app to help residents of your local community find a reason to get together and get that necessary social connection? That is the heart of community after all, and a village without its occupants is a derelict ruin. So, feel free to scroll what's happening in your local community, make friends, stay in touch and keep people connected!

Happy grouping!
